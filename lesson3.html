ExcelDemy ‚Äì How to Convert Decimal to Binary in Excel
Try at least one method from the guide before completing your spreadsheet.
  1. In cell A2, type a number from 0 to 255 (try lots!).
  2. In cells B2:I2, show each bit (MSB ‚Üí LSB) using formulas such as:
